The Zakasi Wavecrawler is a self-sufficient floating city endlessly wandering the sunless depths of zakaas' seas. By day the ship breaches to absorb the solar radiation of Zakaas' sun and recharge its batteries. By night it sinks back below the sea to collect materials from the sea-floor. Zakaas is home to extremely dangerous wildlife, not the least of which are rival Zakasi and their rival ships. For this reason wavecrawlers travel heavily armed, carrying both a sonar cannon for combat with smaller monsters and torpedos for use against sonar-shielded ships and larger monsters.
