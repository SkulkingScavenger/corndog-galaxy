Letkirysa civilization constitutes a powerful combination of technological innovation and shrewd opportunism. Their vast interstellar bureaucracies have neither lasting enemies nor true friends. Despite their nepotistic tendencies, Letkirysa are imminently rational and their civilization has come to incorporate many other species who manage to ingratiate themselves by their loyalty and usefulness. **Government**
The Letkirysa have developed a unique social system since leaving their home planet Kriys. Scientifically referred to as the Letkirysa Corporation nearly the entire species coalesced to form an incorporated entity known as North Shore, occasionally stylized as NorthShore or abbreviated NS. The company is divided into several divisions, each specializing in a specific industry, technology, or some aspect of running the company.
